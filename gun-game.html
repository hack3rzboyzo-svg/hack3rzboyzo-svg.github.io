2D Overhead Shooter

Select the number of players and adjust enemy behavior before starting the match.

Player 1 (Green): WASD to move, mouse to aim, click to shoot.

Player 2 (Pink): Arrow keys to move, mouse to aim, click to shoot.

Player 3 (Gold): IJKL to move, J or L to shoot.

Touchscreen Mode: Use the virtual joysticks below the game area.
